# **App Name**: SkyboundKids Academy

## Core Features:

- Admin Panel & Content Upload: Secure login for administrators to upload and manage lessons, tasks, and quiz content, including text, images, and other media.
- Supabase Integration for Data Storage: Backend integration with Supabase for storing all educational content (lessons, tasks, quizzes) and user-specific progress data.
- Kid-Friendly Lesson Display: The 'Academy' page displays lessons as engaging, visually rich cards with cartoon-inspired design elements, making learning accessible and fun.
- Interactive Quiz System: Children can engage with quizzes, providing multiple-choice answers, and receive immediate feedback on their responses.
- Task & Lab Exploration: Dedicated pages for 'Task' and 'Lab' content, presenting activities and interactive elements for children to explore and complete.
- Personalized Progress Tracking: A 'Profile' page that displays a child's completed lessons and quiz scores, allowing them to track their learning journey.
- AI-Powered Encouragement Tool: After a child completes a lesson or quiz, an AI tool generates a personalized, encouraging message or a fun, context-aware fact to celebrate their achievement.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A vibrant, cheerful violet (#D64CE0) to evoke creativity and positivity, serving as a bright focal point in the design.
- Background color: A very soft, almost-white lavender (#F7EFF8) to provide a light and clean canvas that maintains visual warmth while minimizing eye strain for young users.
- Accent color: A lively, warm rose pink (#F473B3) used for interactive elements, buttons, and highlights, adding playful contrast and energy.
- Headline and body font: 'Poppins' (sans-serif) for its friendly geometric shapes and modern, clean aesthetic, suitable for both titles and readable text blocks for children.
- Icons should feature a distinct cartoonish, soft, and slightly rounded style, reflecting 'Korean diary' aesthetics and themes of clouds, stars, and playful learning to align with the 'Skybound' concept.
- Implement a responsive, mobile-first design with a clear bottom navigation bar for main sections, and utilize card-based layouts for displaying lessons and tasks, mimicking diary entries.
- Incorporate subtle, bouncy transitions and cheerful, simple celebratory animations (e.g., small bursts of stars or confetti) for positive reinforcement upon task completion or page navigation.
